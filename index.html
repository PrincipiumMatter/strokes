The Fine Strokes

Calligraphy & personalized gifts, made one letter at a time

Philosophy

Every commission begins with a blank sheet and a full pot of ink. There is no undo here — only intention. The Fine Strokes exists for the letters, wraps and small objects that are meant to be kept: a name written the way it deserves, a gift that carries a hand in it rather than a barcode.

Offerings

Custom Calligraphy

Names, quotes and monograms hand-lettered to order, in a style matched to the person receiving them.

Personalized Gifts

Small objects — boxes, frames, keepsakes — finished with a hand-written name or line that makes them un-regiftable.

Wedding & Invitation Suites

Wax Seals & Stationery

How it's made
01

Consultation

You share the occasion, the names, the wording — we talk through what it should feel like.

02

Sketch & Proof

A pencil layout comes back to you first, so nothing is inked until you've approved it.

03

Written by Hand

Nib, ink and paper — every piece is lettered individually, no printing or tracing involved.

04

Delivered with Care

Packaged, sealed and sent to you or straight to the person it was written for.

Let's put pen to paper

Tell us the occasion, the name and the date — we'll write back with a proof before anything touches ink.
